"Oasis" Sanatorium in Karkaralinsk

This is an ideal place for rest and wellness surrounded by picturesque nature. Located in the heart of the Karkaralinsk mountains, the sanatorium combines clean mountain air, modern medical procedures, and comfortable living conditions.

Our sanatorium offers a comprehensive approach to health improvement and rehabilitation, using advanced treatment methods and natural factors of the region.

Treatment Directions

Cardiology

Cardiology at the "Oasis" sanatorium is a comprehensive program for the prevention and restoration of the cardiovascular system. Treatment includes hardware and physiotherapeutic procedures, cardio training, therapeutic physical culture, and diet therapy.

Under the supervision of experienced specialists, rehabilitation after heart attacks, normalization of blood pressure, and strengthening of blood vessels are carried out. The clean air of the Karkaralinsk mountains and the calm atmosphere contribute to the effective restoration of the heart and general well-being.

Neurology

Our neurological rehabilitation programs include an individual approach to each patient. We specialize in recovery after strokes, treatment of neurosis, headaches, and other neurological diseases.

The complex of procedures includes physiotherapy, massage, therapeutic exercises, hydrotherapy, and psychotherapeutic sessions aimed at improving the functioning of the nervous system and improving the quality of life.

Musculoskeletal System

Treatment of diseases of the musculoskeletal system in our sanatorium is based on a comprehensive approach. We offer effective methods for recovery after injuries, treatment of arthritis, arthrosis, osteochondrosis, and other pathologies.

The treatment program includes mud therapy, mineral baths, massage, therapeutic exercises, physiotherapy, and modern hardware techniques that contribute to the restoration of mobility and reduction of pain syndrome.

Medical Procedures

  • Mineral and therapeutic baths (pearl, coniferous, hydrogen sulfide)
  • Mud therapy (paraffin therapy, ozokerite applications)
  • Inhalations with herbal and mineral solutions
  • Hydrotherapy (Sharko shower, circular shower, underwater massage)
  • Oxygen therapy (oxygen cocktails, pressure chamber)
  • Physiotherapy procedures (electrophoresis, UHF, magnetotherapy)
  • Massage (classical, acupressure, lymphatic drainage)
  • Reflexology and acupuncture
  • Therapeutic physical training and kinesitherapy
