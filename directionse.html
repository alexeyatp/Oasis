Cardiology

Comprehensive treatment of the cardiovascular system

Cardiology at Oasis Sanatorium is a comprehensive program for the prevention and restoration of the cardiovascular system. Treatment includes hardware and physiotherapeutic procedures, cardio training, therapeutic exercise, and diet therapy.

Under the supervision of experienced specialists, rehabilitation after heart attacks, normalization of blood pressure, and strengthening of blood vessels are carried out. The clean air of the Karkaralinsk mountains and the calm atmosphere contribute to the effective restoration of heart health and general well-being.

Indications for treatment:

  • Ischemic heart disease
  • Arterial hypertension
  • Post-infarction condition
  • Heart rhythm disorders
  • Varicose veins
  • Vegetative-vascular dystonia
  • Prevention of cardiovascular diseases

Neurology

Treatment and restoration of the nervous system

Our neurological rehabilitation programs include an individual approach to each patient. We specialize in recovery after strokes, treatment of neuroses, headaches, and other neurological diseases.

The complex of procedures includes physiotherapy, massage, therapeutic exercises, hydrotherapy, and psychotherapeutic sessions aimed at improving the function of the nervous system and quality of life.

Indications for treatment:

  • Recovery after stroke
  • Neuroses and neurotic conditions
  • Chronic fatigue
  • Migraines and headaches
  • Neuritis and neuralgia
  • Osteochondrosis with neurological manifestations
  • Sleep disorders
  • Stress conditions

Musculoskeletal System

Restoration of mobility and pain relief

Treatment of musculoskeletal system diseases in our sanatorium is based on a comprehensive approach. We offer effective methods for recovery after injuries, treatment of arthritis, arthrosis, osteochondrosis, and other pathologies.

The treatment program includes mud therapy, mineral baths, massage, therapeutic exercises, physiotherapy, and modern hardware techniques that help restore mobility and reduce pain syndrome.

Indications for treatment:

  • Spinal osteochondrosis
  • Arthritis and arthrosis
  • Rheumatic diseases
  • Rehabilitation after injuries
  • Intervertebral hernias
  • Posture disorders
  • Muscle spasms and pain syndromes
  • Prevention of joint diseases

Respiratory System

Improvement and strengthening of the respiratory system

The clean mountain air of the Karkaralinsk mountains combined with modern treatment methods create ideal conditions for the restoration and strengthening of the respiratory system.

Comprehensive treatment includes inhalations with medicinal herbs and mineral solutions, oxygen cocktails, breathing exercises, physiotherapy, and climatotherapy, which have a beneficial effect on the respiratory organs.

Indications for treatment:

  • Chronic bronchitis
  • Bronchial asthma
  • Chronic pneumonia
  • Allergic respiratory diseases
  • Recovery after COVID-19
  • Frequent cold-related illnesses
  • Chronic tonsillitis and pharyngitis
  • Prevention of respiratory system diseases

Gastroenterology

Treatment of gastrointestinal tract diseases

Oasis Sanatorium offers comprehensive programs for the treatment and prevention of gastrointestinal tract diseases, including both medicinal therapy and non-medicinal methods.

Treatment includes mineral waters, mud therapy, physiotherapy, special diet therapy, and therapeutic physical exercises that help normalize digestion and restore the functions of the gastrointestinal tract.

Indications for treatment:

  • Gastritis and gastroduodenitis
  • Peptic ulcer of the stomach and duodenum
  • Colitis and enterocolitis
  • Biliary dyskinesia
  • Chronic cholecystitis
  • Pancreatitis
  • Metabolic disorders
  • Irritable bowel syndrome

Medical Procedures

Mineral and Therapeutic Baths

Pearl, coniferous, and hydrogen sulfide baths improve blood circulation, relieve muscle tension, reduce pain syndrome, and promote general relaxation. They are especially effective for cardiovascular system diseases and musculoskeletal disorders.

Mud Therapy

Paraffin therapy and ozokerite applications provide anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving effects, improve metabolic processes in tissues, and help dissolve scars and adhesions. They are effective in treating joint and spine diseases.

Inhalations

Inhalations with herbal and mineral solutions cleanse the respiratory tract, improve lung ventilation, and have anti-inflammatory effects. They are used for respiratory diseases, ENT disorders, and for prevention of seasonal illnesses.

Hydrotherapy

Sharko shower, circular shower, and underwater massage improve blood circulation, tone muscles, normalize metabolism, and have general strengthening effects. They are effective for sleep disorders, neuroses, and musculoskeletal system diseases.

Oxygen Therapy

Oxygen cocktails and hyperbaric chamber saturate the body with oxygen, improve metabolic processes, boost immunity and performance. They are especially beneficial for chronic fatigue, stress, and cardiovascular diseases.

Physiotherapy

Electrophoresis, UHF, magnetotherapy, and other physiotherapeutic procedures improve microcirculation, have anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects, and accelerate tissue regeneration processes. They are used for a wide range of diseases.
