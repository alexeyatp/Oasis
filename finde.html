How to Reach Us

By Car from Karaganda

Distance: about 220 km

Approximate travel time: 3 hours

Route:

  • Leave Karaganda via the P-187 highway towards Karkaraly town
  • Follow the highway to the Karkaraly sign
  • After passing through the town, follow the signs to Sanatorium "Oasis" (15 km from the town center)

Note: The road is paved throughout. Free parking is provided for guests on the sanatorium grounds.

By Bus

From Karaganda:

Buses depart daily from Karaganda bus station to Karkaraly:

  • Departure times: 08:00, 12:00, 16:00
  • Ticket price: around 2500 tenge
  • Travel time: 3.5-4 hours

From Karkaraly to the sanatorium:

From Karkaraly bus station to the sanatorium, you can reach us by:

  • Taxi: approximately 2000-2500 tenge
  • Route taxi №5: departs every hour, costs 300 tenge

Transfer from the Sanatorium

Sanatorium "Oasis" offers a transfer service for its guests:

  • Meeting at Karaganda airport
  • Transfer from Karkaraly bus station
  • Individual transfer in a comfortable vehicle

To order a transfer:

Contact the booking department at +7 (771) 773-21-34 at least 24 hours before arrival.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times at the sanatorium?
Check-in at the sanatorium is from 14:00, check-out is until 12:00. Subject to availability, early check-in or late check-out is possible for an additional fee.
Are there any attractions nearby?
Yes, in the vicinity of Sanatorium "Oasis" there are numerous natural and historical attractions: Karkaraly National Park, Lake Shaitankol, Aulietas Cave, Kent Mountains. The sanatorium organizes excursions to these sites.
Is internet available on the sanatorium grounds?
Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the sanatorium. However, in some remote parts of the territory, the signal may be weaker due to the mountainous terrain.
Which mobile network works best in the sanatorium area?
Beeline and Kcell provide the most stable coverage in the sanatorium area. The signal of other operators may be unstable due to the features of the mountainous terrain.
Is there medical staff at the sanatorium?
Yes, Sanatorium "Oasis" has qualified medical staff, including therapists, cardiologists, neurologists, and physiotherapists. Medical assistance is available 24/7.
Do I need to bring special clothing?
We recommend bringing comfortable clothing for walking in mountainous terrain, swimwear for the pool and spa procedures, and warm clothes for evening walks (even in summer, it can be cool in the mountains). Special robes and slippers for medical treatments are provided by the sanatorium.

Surroundings of the Sanatorium

Natural Attractions

Unique natural sites are located in the vicinity of Sanatorium "Oasis":

  • Lake Shaitankol - a picturesque mountain lake (7 km from the sanatorium)
  • Karkaraly National Park - a protected natural area with rich flora and fauna
  • Aulietas Cave - an ancient cave with rock paintings (12 km from the sanatorium)
  • "Finger" and "Shanyrak" Rocks - unique natural rock formations

The sanatorium organizes excursions to these places. Information can be obtained at the reception.

Karkaraly Town

In Karkaraly town, located 15 km from the sanatorium, you can visit:

  • Historical and Local History Museum - an exhibition about the history and culture of the region
  • Kunanbay Mosque - a historical religious building from the 19th century
  • Merchant Ryazantsev's House - an architectural monument
  • Central Market - where you can buy local products and souvenirs

The town also has cafes, restaurants, pharmacies, and shops where you can purchase everything you need.
